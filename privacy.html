Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Claude Code Tutorial collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit this website.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly. This website does not use forms, user accounts, or email subscriptions. However, third-party services integrated into this site may collect data as described below.

Local Storage

This website uses your browser's localStorage to remember your language preference (English or Chinese). This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdSense — Serves advertisements on this website. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites.
  • Google Fonts — Provides web fonts for this website. Google may collect anonymous usage data when fonts are loaded.
  • Google Fonts CDN — Serves font files. Your IP address may be logged by Google's servers during font file requests.

AdSense Cookie Disclosure

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or www.aboutads.info.

Cookies

We use a cookie consent banner. A cookie is stored in your browser to remember your consent choice. Google AdSense also sets cookies for ad personalization and measurement. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect ad display.

GDPR / CCPA Rights

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or California, you have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, delete, and port your data. Since we do not collect personal data directly, these rights primarily apply to data held by third-party services. You may opt out of personalized ads via Google's Ads Settings.

Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at privacy@claude-code.tutorial.
